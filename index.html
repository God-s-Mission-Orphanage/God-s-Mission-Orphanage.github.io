The orphanage started in 2011 and is still opened today.
Comprised of 47 orphans, with ages ranging from 2 to 17, the workers of the orphanage are always driven by the compassion that comes with seeing orphans becoming bandits (kulunas in Lingala) when they grow up, especially in Kinshasa where they develop violent mentalities.

"There are children that are always rejected and I get often called by the state to pick them but it's been a while I am refusing to take new children because I barely have enough to feed the ones I have", said Maurice Bosenza, the manager of the orphanage.
